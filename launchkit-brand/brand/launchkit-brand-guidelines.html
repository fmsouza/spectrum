LaunchKit is the hub between coding-agent harnesses and every LLM provider. The identity makes that literal: one steady anchor, a spectrum of providers fanning out from it.

01 — Brand essence

Five pillars, one hub

The mark is a routing hub: an incoming request on the left, a glowing anchor core, and a fan of provider endpoints in the spectrum hues. Every brand decision ladders back to it.

Modern

Clean geometry, dark-first surfaces, and a typeface (Geist) built for software.

Bleeding-edge

A living spectrum of providers and models — the brand flexes as the ecosystem moves.

Professional

Restrained color, real hierarchy, nothing gimmicky. It reads like infrastructure you trust.

Reliable

One anchor blue, consistent tokens, predictable behavior. The hub always holds.

Flexible

Four accent hues stand in for many providers — categorical, recomposable, never locked in.

02 — Logo

The routing-hub mark

A combination mark: the hub symbol plus the LaunchKit wordmark set in Geist Bold, with “Kit” carrying the anchor blue. The symbol stands alone as the app, tray, and favicon icon.

Primary lockup · on graphite

App icon · squircle tile

On light surfaces

Monochrome · knockout

Favicon · simplified

Do

  • Keep clear space ≥ the hub’s diameter on all sides.
  • Use the colour mark on graphite or white; the knockout on solid brand fills.
  • Minimum sizes: mark 20 px, lockup 120 px wide.
  • Write it “LaunchKit” — one word, capital L and K.

Don’t

  • Recolour the hub or swap the spectrum endpoints arbitrarily.
  • Stretch, rotate, outline, or add shadows/glows to the mark.
  • Place the colour lockup on busy or low-contrast imagery.
  • Spell it “Launchkit”, “Launch Kit”, or “LAUNCHKIT”.

05 — Voice & tone

Precise, not flashy

LaunchKit talks like good infrastructure: confident, exact, and quiet. It explains what it does and gets out of the way. Technical, never cold; opinionated, never hype.

We sound like

  • “Route any coding agent to any LLM.”
  • “One proxy on loopback. Your keys never leave the OS keychain.”
  • “Map an alias. Launch a harness. Done.”

Not like

  • “The world’s most revolutionary AI super-platform!”
  • “Unlock infinite possibilities with next-gen synergy.”
  • Emoji-laden, exclamation-heavy hype.
